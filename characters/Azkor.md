## Активная способность: Overwhelming Wrath
Until the beginning of his next turn Azkor Taunts all adjacent enemies and takes -X% Damage from ranged attacks. Deals 2x Y Eviscerating Damage to all adjacent enemies at the beginning of Azkor's next turn.

## Пассивная способность: Beacon of Rage
For each time he was attacked in battle, up to a maximum of 8 times, Azkor deals +X Damage. If Azkor has defeated an enemy in this battle, 50% of the extra damage granted by Beacon of Rage is added to the damage of melee attacks of adjacent friendly Chaos units.
