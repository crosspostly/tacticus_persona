# Ahriman

**Фракция:** Chaos (Thousand Sons)
**Редкость:** Legendary

## Базовые статы
- Здоровье: 80
- Броня: 18
- Урон: 30

## Атаки
- Ближний бой: Flame / 1 hit
- Дальний бой: Psychic / 1 hit / Range 2
- Передвижение: 3

## Черты
- Flying
- Psyker
- Weaver of Fates

## Активная способность: Doombolt
Deals 3x X Psychic Damage, evenly distributing the hits between all enemies within 2 hexes. Adds +1 Hit for each hex on the battlefield that is currently on Fire. Cannot exceed 9 Hits. When Ahriman defeats an enemy with his normal ranged attack and Doombolt has already been used, then Doombolt can be used again in this battle. Note: Doombolt will recharge each time the condition is met.

## Пассивная способность: Psychic Stalk
Attacks that deal Flame Damage and Psychic Damage to enemies that are on hexes with Fire deal +X% Damage. Affected attacks from friendly Chaos units deal an additional +Y Damage. The hex of each enemy that is hit by Ahriman's normal ranged attack catches Fire. Note: The bonus applies per hit and the Chaos unit bonus is added before the percent bonus is calculated.
