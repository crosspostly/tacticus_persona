## Активная способность: Vexilla Magnifica
Deals 1x X Physical Damage to an adjacent enemy target и an additional adjacent enemy unit. Both enemies are Stunned for 1 round, and Aesoth receives -Y% Damage for 1 round. This ability can be used multiple times per battle but has a cooldown of 2 rounds.

## Пассивная способность: Stand Vigil
Friendly surrounding units have +X Armour and deal +Y% Damage with attacks that are not normal attacks. If a friendly Adeptus Custodes uses an Active Ability, Stand Vigil affects all other friendly units within 2 hexes for 1 round.
