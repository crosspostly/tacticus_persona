## Активная способность: Drach'nyen
Abaddon is set to X Health and immediatley performs a normal melee attack against an adjacent enemy. After this attack and for the rest of the battle, after performing a normal melee attack, Abaddon performs a second attack against an adjacent enemy, dealing 3x Y Piercing Damage.

## Пассивная способность: First Among Traitors
Other friendly Chaos units within 2 hexes deal +X Damage. This value is increased by +Y Damage for each attack Abaddon has performed in this battle, up to a maximum of Z Damage.
