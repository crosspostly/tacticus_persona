- Урон: 14

- Передвижение: 5

## Черты
- Deep Strike
- Flying

## Активная способность: Swooping Hawk
Flies over and deals a wave of damage from an initial melee target to targets up to two hexes behind. 1x X Piercing Damage to the first target, 1x Y Piercing Damage to the second, and 1x Z Piercing Damage to the third. Lands in the hex directly behind the last target hit, or - in case that hex is occupied - to the closest free hex to it. This ability's damage ignores all of the attacker bonuses and modifiers (including elevation), and cannot Crit.

## Пассивная способность: Path of Command
Other friendly units within 2 hexes gain +X Damage. Their Crit chance - if they have one - is increased by +Y%. Affected Aeldari units gain an additional +Z Damage.
