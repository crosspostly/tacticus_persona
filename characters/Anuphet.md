# Anuphet

**Фракция:** Necrons
**Редкость:** Epic

## Базовые статы
- Здоровье: 85
- Броня: 19
- Урон: 17

## Атаки
- Ближний бой: Energy / 4 hits
- Дальний бой: Energy / 3 hits / Range 2
- Передвижение: 3

## Черты
- Living Metal

## Активная способность: Resurrection Orb
In a free adjacent hex, summons 1 Necron Warrior for each round that has started. Summons 1 more Necron Warrior for each friendly unit with the trait Living Metal that was defeated in this battle. Reanimation Protocols (Passive) If there is at least one other friendly unit of the same type on the battlefield when this unit is defeated, it has a 35% chance to be immediately revived with 25% of its initial Health in the same hex or a random adjacent one. Does not apply if Overkilled.

## Пассивная способность: Martial Apotheosis
At the start of its turn, Anuphet deals 2x X Energy Damage to a random adjacent enemy.
