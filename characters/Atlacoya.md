## Активная способность: Talons of the Emperor
Deals 1x X Power Damage to an adjacent enemy unit. This attack deals +Y Damage for each time a friendly Character has used an active ability this battle. If the target is a Psyker or Atlacoya is adjacent to a friendly Adeptus Custodes, this attack deals Direct Damage instead.
