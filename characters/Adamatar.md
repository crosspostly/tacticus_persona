## Черты

## Активная способность: Doom Siren
Deals 3x X Direct Damage to an adjacent enemy and up to 2 other random enemies adjacent to the target. For the rest of the battle, Adamatar's normal attacks score an additional Hit for each enemy Overkilled by Doom Siren.

## Пассивная способность: Obsessive Annunciation
All enemy units adjacent to Adamatar take +X Damage from ranged attacks. If an enemy was Overkilled this turn, enemies adjacent to Adamatar take +X Crit Damage from all attacks by friendly Chaos units.
