## Активная способность: Foehammer
Attack target unit within range 2 for 1x X Power Damage and the target is Stunned until the beginning of Arjac's next turn. For each Character that has been defeated in this battle, this ability deals +Y Damage.

## Пассивная способность: Anvil Shield
Arjac blocks an additional X Damage and when he Blocks he covers the attacker's Hex in Ice. If Arjac is below 35% Health, or if his attacker is on a Hex covered in Ice, he gains +Y% Block Chance and if he blocks a melee attack, the attacker is Stunned until the end of the attacker's next turn.
